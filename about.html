Evolution

How Attendant Scaled with Intention

Our growth has never been about adding noise. Each chapter expanded geographic reach, operational depth, and the level of trust clients place in us.

2018

The London launch

Attendant began as a small concierge studio serving a handful of leadership teams who needed premium, deeply personal operational support.

2019

Expansion into New York

Demand from finance, legal, and client-service firms pushed Attendant into a second hub built for speed, discretion, and high-stakes response work.

2021

Security became a core differentiator

ISO 27001 certification formalized what clients already expected: enterprise-grade controls wrapped around a human support model.

2022

The digital layer arrived

A dedicated portal gave employees one elegant front door for requests, while concierges kept the white-glove service experience behind it.

2023

Global continuity through Singapore

Opening the Asia hub allowed Attendant to support multinational teams with round-the-clock continuity and faster local execution.

Today

From executive perk to company-wide advantage

Attendant now helps organizations turn personal logistics into a strategic employee benefit, giving back time where it matters most.

The next chapter is built around calmer, higher-performing teams.
